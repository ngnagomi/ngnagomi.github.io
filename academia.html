Colorimetric Assay using Unmodified Gold Nanoparticles for Label-free Detection of COVID-19

Nagomi Gopinath

International Young Researchers' Conference

Abstract This research was focused on developing a sensitive gold nanoparticle-aptamer mediated colorimetric assay to identify the SARS CoV-2 spike protein S2 (CoV-S2) and the sensing system detects as low as ~109 copies/mL of CoV-S2. The negative control with CoV-S1 and nucleocapsid protein did not show any color changes, indicating the specific detection of CoV-S2. This colorimetric assay helps to identify the lower level of CoV-S2, and discriminates other related proteins, which helps to diagnose COVID-19 at the initial stages of infection.
